FEATURING Yetunde Olagbaju

SWEPT UP FROM ARIES TO PISCES TO THE AGE OF AQUARIUS -
PAST PRESENT & FUTURE

The Equinox is nothing more than an exact moment in time where things happen to line up in the universe. This event, like many others in our lives, reminds us of and, furthermore, reaffirms our existence in space and time. With this event in mind, I began to think of my history and when my identity and family time line has happened to line up in my mind. In the "EQUINOX" audio I explored creating and retelling these instances of identity revelations and how (like when we look at the sky) we often get lost in our own time and history.

I wanted to visually represent my ideas in the audio. Concepts of history, instances of alignment. Thoughts of "myself" in the past (Aries), present (Pisces), and future (Aquarius). What did I look like? Where do I fit in? What will I become? All of the constellations line up together over a faceless space wind wept figure.
